Site Name: Wasatch Tracker

I picked Wasatch Tracker because this website tracks snow conditions at ski resorts in Utah, all of which are in the Wasatch Mountain range.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to track snow conditions of each ski resort in Utah, as well as what the forecast is for the next ten days. In addition, users can see what price of season passes and day tickets are for each resort.

Scenarios

Where can I see all Utah ski resort snow conditions all in one place?

What resort is the best for my difficulty level?
